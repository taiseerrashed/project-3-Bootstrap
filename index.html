Popular destinations in Egypt

Major tourist destinations include the millennia-old monuments in the Nile Valley. Principal among them are the pyramids and Great Sphinx at Giza.

Luxor about 500 km south of Cairo, is the site of the ancient city of Thebes. It includes the ruins of the temple complexes at Karnak and Luxor, which stand within the modern and the opposite side of the Nile River lie the monuments. templs and tombs on the West Bank Necropolis, which include the valley of the Kings and valley of the Queens.

Sinai has the beach resorts of Sharm el-Sheikh, Dahab, Nuweiba and Taba as well as locations mentioned in the Bible such as Mount Sinai ("Jabal Mosa") Sant Catherine's Monastery may be the oldest working Christian monastery in the world.
